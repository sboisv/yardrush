How it works
Post. Pick up. Done.

No agencies, no middlemen. Post what you need or browse jobs in your neighbourhood and help a neighbour out. Payments are arranged directly between community members.

1
Post a job
Describe what you need, choose a service type, and post it to the community board for your neighbours to see.
2
A neighbour picks it up
Community members browse open jobs and claim the ones they can help with. You'll be notified when someone picks up your job.
3
Coordinate directly
Connect and coordinate directly with your neighbour. No middleman, no agency — just two community members working it out.
4
Leave a review
Rate the experience after every job. Payment is arranged directly between you and your neighbour. Reviews build community trust.

YardRush is currently in Beta. Features and services may evolve as we grow. YardRush is a community-led platform that connects neighbours looking for help with those willing to offer it. Anyone in the community can post or pick up a job. YardRush does not directly provide services, does not verify or screen community members, and does not certify any work performed. YardRush is not responsible for the quality, adequacy, or completion of any job arranged through the platform. YardRush is not liable for any injury, accident, damage to persons or property, or loss of any kind that may occur during or as a result of any job arranged through the platform. All community members participate at their own risk. Use good judgment and stay safe.
